Legziz Fashion Boutique

Style that Speaks. Identity that Shines.

1. Site Name

Site Name: Legziz Fashion Boutique

This name was chosen to reflect a modern, stylish, and culturally grounded fashion brand that highlights African elegance and urban identity. It's unique, brandable, and speaks to fashion-conscious audiences.

Optional domain: Legzizboutique.com (availability check optional)

2. Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to showcase and sell clothing items made by Legziz Fashion Boutique. It will include:

  • Product catalog with high-quality images
  • Contact and order information
  • Fashion inspiration blog or lookbook
  • Online ordering and delivery options

3. Scenarios

  • Where can I view the latest arrivals in men's and women's fashion?
  • How do I place an order or contact the boutique for a custom design?

4. Color Schema

  • Purple (#8A2BE2): Used for headings, buttons, and key accents to convey luxury and creativity.
  • Light Grey (#f5f5f5): Used for background to provide a clean and minimalistic look.

5. Typography

  • Georgia: Used for headings to give a classic and elegant look.
  • Segoe UI: Used for body text for clean readability and modern appeal.
